About MedicalPredict

MedicalPredict is an AI-powered prediction platform designed to estimate individual medical insurance costs in seconds. By analyzing key health indicators such as Age, BMI, Smoking Habits, and Region, it delivers quick and reliable cost predictions using advanced machine learning models.

This project helps both individuals and insurance companies better understand the impact of lifestyle and demographic factors on healthcare costs, promoting smarter financial and medical decisions.

Built with Flask, Python, and Machine Learning, this tool demonstrates the power of data-driven decision-making in real-world applications.
